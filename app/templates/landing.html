Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a Clipper

How do payouts work?

You submit your Telegram/phone contacts. Once a manager approves your clip, they reach out personally to confirm details and send PayPal/bank transfer manually.

Is there a review backlog?

Our average review time is under 24 hours. Managers triage by KPI urgency and send feedback in Telegram if edits are required.

What platforms are supported?

We're focused on TikTok and Instagram Reels, with Shorts pilots launching soon. Each brief specifies the target channel.

How much can I earn per clip?

Payouts range from €50-€300 per approved clip depending on complexity, brand tier, and usage rights. Premium campaigns can pay up to €500.

Do I need professional equipment?

Not necessarily. Many successful clippers use smartphones. What matters most is creativity, understanding trends, and following brief requirements.

What's the approval process like?

Submit your portfolio/social handles. Our team reviews your content quality and style. Most applications get a response within 48 hours.

Can I work with multiple brands simultaneously?

Absolutely! Once approved, you can access all available briefs and submit to multiple brands as long as you meet each campaign's requirements.

What if my clip gets rejected?

Managers provide detailed feedback via Telegram. You can revise and resubmit if the brief is still active, or apply the feedback to future submissions.

Are there any fees or commissions?

No hidden fees. You receive the full payout amount specified in each brief. We handle payment processing costs.

How do I get started?

Click "Become a Clipper" to register. Complete your profile, submit portfolio samples, and once approved, you'll get access to available briefs immediately.

Is this available outside the DACH region?

While we focus on DACH brands, we accept clippers globally. However, some briefs may require local language skills or market knowledge.

What kind of brands do you work with?

We partner with premium DACH brands across fashion, tech, lifestyle, beauty, and entertainment sectors. All campaigns are manually vetted for quality.
