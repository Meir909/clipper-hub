Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Clipper

How do payouts work?

You submit your Telegram/phone contacts. Once a manager approves your clip, they reach out personally to confirm details and send PayPal/bank transfer manually.

Is there a review backlog?

Our average review time is under 24 hours. Managers triage by KPI urgency and send feedback in Telegram if edits are required.

What platforms are supported?

We currently support TikTok and Instagram Reels. Each brief specifies platform requirements and optimal formats.

How are earnings calculated?

Each campaign has a fixed payout per approved clip. Rates typically range from €15-€300 depending on brand and complexity.

Can I work from any country?

We focus on DACH creators (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) but welcome talented creators from across Europe who understand the market.
